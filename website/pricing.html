Pricing FAQ

MaidHub is laser-focused on solo cleaning business owners. One plan means we can make it exceptional rather than spreading ourselves thin. No confusing tiers, no feature paywalls — just everything you need, at one honest price.

No caps, period. Unlimited clients, unlimited service addresses, unlimited jobs, unlimited invoices. Whether you have 5 clients or 50, the price stays the same and nothing gets restricted.

Sign up and get 30 days of full, unrestricted access — no credit card required. Every feature is unlocked from day one. At the end of your trial you'll be asked if you'd like to subscribe. If not, your account pauses with your data intact.

We offer a 7-day grace period after a failed payment. Your account stays fully active during this window and we'll send you a reminder. Your data is always preserved — we'll never delete it due to a missed payment.

You can cancel at any time — no long-term contracts, no cancellation fees. We don't offer partial-month refunds, but your access continues until the end of your current billing period. We keep it simple and fair.
