Job Scheduling

Recurring jobs, automated. Your schedule runs itself.

Set up a weekly client once and MaidHub fills in every future appointment automatically. Your calendar stays full without you lifting a finger.

  • Set recurring jobs once — daily, weekly, biweekly, or monthly
  • Auto-generate future appointments up to 12 months out
  • Day, week, and month calendar views
  • Quick reschedule or cancel with one tap — no double-booking
Estimates

Build a quote in 60 seconds flat.

Stop guessing your price in a text message. Build a polished, itemized estimate and send it directly to your client — they confirm with a click.

  • Pre-saved service items for lightning-fast quoting
  • Real-time total calculation as you add line items
  • Send by email or SMS — clients approve in one tap
  • Convert approved estimates to jobs automatically

Invoicing

From job complete to paid — in seconds.

Send a professional invoice the moment a job is done. MaidHub tracks what's paid, what's pending, and what's overdue — so you never have to wonder where your money is.

  • Auto-generate invoices from completed jobs
  • Track paid, unpaid, and overdue at a glance
  • Send reminders to late payers automatically
  • Accept payment links via Stripe — no extra apps needed
Notifications

Your clients are always in the loop.

Automated reminders mean no more last-minute "wait, was that today?" texts. MaidHub pings your clients 24 hours before every appointment — and confirms when you're done.

  • 24-hour appointment reminders by email or SMS
  • "Job complete" notifications with photo attachments
  • Invoice-due reminders sent automatically
  • Fully customizable templates — your tone, your words
