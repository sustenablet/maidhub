FAQ

Frequently asked
questions

MaidHub is fully mobile-optimized and works great in any browser on your phone or tablet — no download required. Native iOS and Android apps are on the roadmap and coming soon.

After your 30-day free trial, you'll need an active subscription to continue using MaidHub. The Solo Cleaner Plan is $29/month. No credit card is required to start your trial — you only enter payment when you're ready to subscribe.

CSV bulk import is coming very soon. For now, clients are added manually — which takes about 1–2 minutes each. Most solo cleaners have 10–20 active clients, so getting started takes under 30 minutes.

Yes. MaidHub is built on Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS) enabled — meaning your data is completely isolated and invisible to any other user. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Phase 1 of MaidHub is built specifically for the solo cleaning business owner — one person, one account. Team management features (assign jobs to multiple cleaners, staff schedules, etc.) are planned for a future release.

You can cancel anytime — no contracts, no cancellation fees. Your account and data remain accessible until the end of your current billing period. Data export is available on request.

In the current MVP, MaidHub handles invoice generation and payment status tracking (mark as paid / pending). Direct integration with Stripe, Square, or other payment processors is on the roadmap for a future update.
