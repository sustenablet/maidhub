Privacy Policy

How MaidHub collects, uses, and protects information when you use the solo cleaner OS.

Last updated: March 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MaidHub (the “Service”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information in connection with your use of our software for solo cleaning business owners.

1. Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to:

  • Solo cleaners and business owners who create an account on MaidHub.
  • Any end-customer data (clients, addresses, jobs) you choose to store in MaidHub.

2. Information we collect

2.1. Account information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name and email address.
  • Business name and phone number (if provided).
  • Authentication data managed by our provider (Supabase Auth), such as password hashes and verification status.

2.2. Subscription and billing

For paid subscriptions (via Square), we store:

  • Square customer and subscription identifiers.
  • Subscription plan, status, and relevant billing events (e.g., start, cancellation, payment failures).

We do not store your full payment card number. Card data is handled by Square under their own terms and privacy policy.

2.3. Business data you enter

To run your business inside MaidHub, you may store:

  • Client contact details (names, phone numbers, email addresses).
  • Service addresses and notes (e.g., gate codes, pet notes, preferences).
  • Jobs, recurring rules, estimates, invoices, and internal notes.
  • Notification logs (email/SMS reminders and confirmations).

2.4. Usage and device information

We may collect limited technical data, such as:

  • Browser type, device type, and operating system.
  • IP address and approximate region.
  • Pages viewed and actions taken within the app (for performance and reliability).

3. How we use your information

We use information to:

  • Provide the Service – authenticate you, store your data, and show you your schedule, clients, and invoices.
  • Process subscriptions – manage trials, check subscription status, and respond to Square webhooks.
  • Send communications – transactional emails/SMS such as reminders, confirmations, and important account notices.
  • Improve reliability – monitor performance, debug issues, and prevent abuse.

4. Legal bases (if you’re in the EU/UK)

Where applicable, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Contract – processing necessary to provide you with the Service you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests – improving the product, preventing abuse, and securing the platform.
  • Consent – where we explicitly ask for it (for example, certain marketing communications).

5. How your data is stored and protected

  • Your data is stored in a managed PostgreSQL database via Supabase.
  • We use Row Level Security (RLS) so each account can only access its own records.
  • Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest by our infrastructure providers.

6. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited data with:

  • Infrastructure providers (e.g., Supabase, hosting, email and SMS providers) that help us deliver the Service.
  • Payment processor (Square) for subscription billing.
  • Legal or compliance recipients when required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

7. Data retention

  • We keep your account and associated business data while your account is active.
  • If you cancel, we may retain data for a reasonable period for backup, legal, or accounting purposes.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of certain data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@maidhub.io.

9. Cookies and similar technologies

MaidHub may use cookies or similar technologies to:

  • Keep you signed in securely.
  • Remember basic preferences.

We currently do not run third-party advertising trackers.

10. International transfers

Depending on your location, your information may be processed in countries outside your own (for example, where our infrastructure providers host data). We rely on appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) where required.

11. Children’s data

MaidHub is not directed to children under 16 and is intended for business use by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top and, where appropriate, notify you in the app or by email.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or how we handle data, contact us at:

Email: privacy@maidhub.io
